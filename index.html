The Craft Behind

Hair restoration is more than a procedure — it's an art form. Our board-certified surgeons combine 18 years of clinical expertise with an obsessive attention to aesthetic detail, ensuring every hairline we craft looks undeniably natural.

Using the latest motorised FUE and DHI techniques, we achieve minimal scarring, accelerated healing, and graft survival rates that consistently exceed the industry standard.

How It Works

Your Journey to Restoration

01
Free Consultation

A thorough assessment of your hair loss stage, donor density, and personal goals. No obligation, complete transparency.

02
Bespoke Treatment Plan

Your surgeon crafts a custom plan including graft count, hairline design, and the optimal technique for your anatomy.

03
Procedure Day

Performed under local anaesthesia in our state-of-the-art theatre. Most patients are relaxed and comfortable throughout.

04
Recovery & Growth

Initial shedding is normal. Visible results emerge from month 4, with full density achieved by month 12–18.

05
Lifetime Aftercare

Ongoing follow-ups, a dedicated patient portal, and complementary PRP sessions to protect your long-term results.
